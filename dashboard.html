Latest E-Waste News & Facts

Gujarat's New E-Waste Recycling Policy

The Gujarat government has launched a policy to boost e-waste recycling and awareness. More collection centers are being set up to handle electronic waste properly.

India Ranks 3rd in Global E-Waste Production

India generates over 3 million metric tons of e-waste annually, but only 20% is recycled properly. Urgent action is needed to prevent further damage to the environment.

Ahmedabad's Record E-Waste Collection

Ahmedabad collected over 50,000 kg of e-waste in a year, setting a record in Gujarat for responsible electronic waste disposal.

E-Waste Contains Toxic Materials

Electronic waste contains hazardous substances like lead and mercury. Improper disposal leads to contamination of water and soil.

Recycling One Phone Saves 20,000 Liters of Water

Studies reveal that recycling a single mobile phone can prevent contamination of up to 20,000 liters of water, protecting our environment.
